Carded In
AMHA,AMHR,ASPC,ASPR and MODERN SHETLANDS

Judging has been a highlight of my life. I have seen so many beautiful horses, met so many wonderful people and been to places I would have never had the chance to experience. My judging has taken me to places like New Zealand, Australia, Belgium, Canada and places all over the United States. I have enjoyed them all from the little back yard show to the National Show in New Zealand. I have also given clinics in several of these places. I feel a real sense of responsibility to the exhibitors and their horses to do the best job I can to judge their horses fairly and honestly.
