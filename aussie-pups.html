Aussie pups

SIERRA RANCH RAISES ONLY QUALITY REGISTERED ASCA AUSTRALIAN SHEPHERDS!

In developing our particular Australian Shepherd bloodline we not only wanted a quick thinking, athletic, working dog, we wanted the beauty of a show dog as well. So we have combined two excellent pedigrees to come up with the best of both worlds.

Our dogs have such Show Champion greats as Ch. Brigadoon's One Arrogant Dude, and many of the famous working dog Champions of Twin Oaks in their bloodlines.

We have been raising Aussie's for over 25 years and feel we have some of the most beautiful dogs you will see anywhere. Their temperament is outstanding. Their intelligence is incredible and their loyalty is divine. They have some of the best pedigrees in the country.

Our dogs work for us on the ranch and truly show what they were bred for.
