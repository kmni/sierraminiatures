Scott Creek High Roller

Previous Next

Sire: Little Kings Napoleon
Dam: Kentucky Mini Horses Derby Eve
Foal Date: 4/20/1993 to 12/5/2011
Color: Black Stallion
Height: 29.5"

This little stallion had it all, color size, and movement with a show record to prove it. He was Champion Liberty Horse in Reno with over 60 horses competing. AMHA National Top Ten Liberty Horse, Supreme Champion Halter Horse, AMHA Register of Merit Horse, AMHA Honor Roll Horse, Norcal High Point Champion in Color, Halter, and Liberty. His offspring carry on his beautiful action, color and conformation.

So much horse in such a small package of only 29.50 inches. He was one of the great ones!
