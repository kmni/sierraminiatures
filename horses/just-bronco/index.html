Just Bronco

Previous Next

Sire: Just Brandy, 31.5"
Dam: Al Mars Golden Girl, 33.5"
Foal Date: 5/18/1983
Color: Buckskin Stallion

Just Bronco was one of our first stallions here on the ranch and although he is getting up in years, he still remains one of the best producing stallions we have, and one of our favorites.

Bronco is a Supreme Champion winner, AMHA National Reserve Champion Color Horse, AMHA National Top Ten Halter Horse (4th)

This Halter and Color Champion has a wonderful temperament, gorgeous head and correct conformation. He is very prepotent and well known for passing on his exceptional qualities to his offspring.
