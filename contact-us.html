DIRECTIONS TO SIERRA RANCH:

Coming up I-80:
 Follow the signs to Reno until you reach Auburn. Take Hwy 49 toward Grass Valley approximately 11 miles out of Auburn (at signal) go left on Wolf Road. Go 4 miles to a T. Go left at the T (Garden Bar) go 3 miles. You will make a sharp right and cross a one-lane bridge. Next road is Sanford turn left. Go left at the Y onto Tierra Road. Sierra Ranch is on the left.>

From Yuba City area:
Take Hwy 20 to Grass Valley - then take Hwy 49 toward Auburn for approximately 11 miles. Turn right on Wolf Road and then follow the instructions above.
