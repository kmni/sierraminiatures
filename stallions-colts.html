Sierra Buckeroos Bay Sensation

dob: 5/28/13
color: Bay Colt

$1600

Sin is a son of our beautiful buckskin stallion Little Kings George W Buck, who is a direct son of the famous Boones Little Buckeroo. Buckeroos dam (Johnstons Vanilla) is by the very well known Gold Melody Boy. George W Buck's dam (Little Kings Contessa) is by Little Kings Buckwheat, another well known stallion. Sin's dam, our wonderful sweet mare Miss Bay, is a direct daughter of Boones Little Buckeroo. Miss Bay is also an AMHA World Champion. So this little colt has some of the best bloodlines you will find in the entire country! To boot, Sin has personality plus! He loves people and is a real sweetheart.
